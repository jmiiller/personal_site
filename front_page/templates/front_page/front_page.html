About Me

Hello! I'm Jeff, an aspiring software engineer, data vizzer, and graduating University of British Columbia Computer Science and Philosophy student currently based in Vancouver, Canada.

I have my sights set on becoming excellent in Full Stack Web Development. I am currently immersed in learning the ins and outs of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, D3, Python, and Django. I also bring sixteen months of prior software development experience, where I was involved with configuring Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery systems and building things with Java.

One thing of great importance to me is the ability to contribute to a product and organization whose values align with my own and whose product is aimed at addressing meaningful issues in society.

One such value I hold is a commitment to the pursuit and preservation of truth, which I believe underlies a healthy society. It is why I studied Philosophy, a discipline that holds the pursuit of truth through rigorous logical analysis and argument as its highest aim. I believe free, independent journalism is essential to attaining that goal.

But it goes without saying that there are an expansive list of other meaningful issues in society, each worthy of attention in their own right. We are all tied to the common project of creating and preserving a free, democratic society that meets the needs of all. I want to do my part to fulfill that project.

I am currently on the hunt for full-time positions. My starting date is flexible.
